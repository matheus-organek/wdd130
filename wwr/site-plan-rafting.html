Overview

Purpose

The objective of the website is to create a means by which customers can find us more easily to have more practical and faster access to our services and information. This will increase our income and also spread information about Rafting.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Families who like a safe dose of adrenaline.

What do they want? They want a place where they can find adequate and safe information about the sport they love. They want to hear stories and adventures from people who have already participated in our company and also want to have this experience.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Questions about safety and procedures to avoid serious accidents, adventurous trips, knowledge about the history of the sport.
