Our mission is to provide families with quality moments with a dose of adrenaline.

HISTORY

It all started a few years ago, when the company's founder wanted to spend quality time with his family, but was tired of going to the mall or the cinema. So he had the idea of ​​rafting with his children and wife, and they loved every second of the experience. Motivated to provide this valuable experience to other families, the founder began researching the rafting market and the needs of families who wanted to branch out from the same normal activities, and founded his own rafting company: Rafting Adventure.
